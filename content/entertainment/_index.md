## Engineering the Impossible

For twenty years, SimpleMotion has engineered motion control systems for live entertainment — from intimate theatre to large-scale touring productions. We design, build, and commission stage machinery that performers and crew trust every night.

## What We Do

- **Stage Machinery** — Fixed and touring automation systems including flying, revolves, lifts, and tracked wagons.
- **Performer Flying** — Safety-rated flying systems for performers and scenic elements, designed to the highest standards.
- **Control Systems** — Purpose-built control platforms (Simplicity) that give operators precise, reliable, and safe command of complex motion cues.
- **Touring Systems** — Robust, road-ready automation packages designed to survive the rigours of touring while maintaining precision.

## Track Record

Our systems have powered hundreds of performances across Australia and internationally, including complex multi-axis touring productions with demanding safety requirements.
