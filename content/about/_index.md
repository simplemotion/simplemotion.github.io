## Our Story

SimpleMotion began in 2006 as Q Motion Automation, founded on the belief that motion control in live entertainment deserved the same engineering rigour as any safety-critical system. In 2016 we became SimpleMotion, reflecting our growing reach across architecture, entertainment, and industry.

In 2026 we celebrate twenty years of engineering motion — from single-axis theatre hoists to complex multi-axis touring automation systems powering hundreds of performances worldwide.

## What We Believe

- **Safety is non-negotiable.** Every system we build is designed for the safety of performers, crew, and the public.
- **Simplicity is strength.** Complex systems should be simple to operate. That principle drives everything from our control software to our documentation.
- **Engineering is a service.** We succeed when our clients can rely on their systems night after night, year after year.

## SimpleMotion.Global Pty Ltd

SimpleMotion.Global Pty Ltd is an Australian engineering company headquartered in Auburn, New South Wales. We are a registered engineering practice with Engineers Australia.
